# ProfitPilot - Free Business Management Software

ProfitPilot is a comprehensive business management platform designed for ambitious entrepreneurs. It provides tools for sales tracking, inventory management, profit & loss analysis, and comprehensive business analytics. **Completely free to use—no credit card required.**

## Features

### 💰 Sales Management
- Daily sales tracking with detailed records
- Sales analytics and trends
- Sales history and reporting
- Quick sales entry interface

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Real-time inventory tracking
- Stock level monitoring
- Product details and categorization
- Low stock alerts
- Inventory reports

### 📊 Financial Reports
- Profit & Loss (P&L) statements
- Daily revenue reports
- Expense tracking
- Revenue analysis
- Financial summaries

### 📈 Business Dashboard
- Real-time analytics
- Key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Visual charts and graphs
- Business overview
- Quick access to important metrics

### 🎓 Interactive Tutorial
- Comprehensive guides for all features
- Step-by-step walkthroughs
- Video tutorials
- FAQ section
- Live support resources

## Getting Started

### 1. Landing Page
Visit the landing page to learn about ProfitPilot and explore its features.

### 2. Login
- Enter your 8-character access code
- No email or password required
- Enjoy secure, simple authentication

### 3. Business Setup
On your first login, set up your business:
- Business name
- Business type
- Location
- Currency preference
- Initial settings

### 4. Dashboard Access
After setup, access the main dashboard with:
- Daily sales tracking
- Inventory management
- Financial reports
- Business analytics
- Help and tutorials

## Navigation
